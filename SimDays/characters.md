## Design principles

- **Discovery, not a menu.** You meet people by being in their place at the right time — no master NPC list shown to the player.
- **Each character has a "where + when".** A schedule (which district, which days/hours) so the world feels alive and you plan around it.
- **Mixed cast.** Male and female, romance *and* friendship *and* rivalry/mentor — not everyone is a date.
- **Hobbies/interests drive content.** Each person has 2–3 interests → gift ideas, conversation topics that land, and date/hangout spots they like.
- **Two meters:** Affection (0–100) and Trust (0–100), separate. Scenes unlock at thresholds + stat checks (CHR/APP/INT…).
- **Relationship types:** Friend · Romance · Rival→Friend · Mentor · Business partner · Antagonist arc.

## ⭐ The roommate — early-game helper (🆕 headline new character)

**Marcus "Marc" Reyes** — M, ~27. Lives in the studio next door (same Apartments block you start in).

- **Met:** Day 1, knocks on your door to welcome you (scripted intro).
- **Role:** onboarding buddy / mentor-lite. Explains the city, nudges you toward jobs, lends you small money once if you go broke, texts you tips.
- **Job:** bartender at **Static** (so he bridges you into nightlife later).
- **Hobbies:** basketball at the park, cooking, old action movies.
- **Relationship:** Friendship (bro storyline). Not romanceable (or optional — open question).
- **Schedule:** mornings/early afternoon at home or park; evenings at the bar.
- **Convo paths:** "settling in" → "finding work" → "his own dream" (wants to open his own bar — mirrors Zoe's arc; you can help or not).
- **Events:** Day 1 welcome · first-week "let me show you around" (unlocks a map tip) · mid-game "Marc needs a hand at the bar" · late "backing his bar" choice.

## Relationship arc structure (proposed)

1. **Stranger** → first meeting (scripted, location-gated).
2. **Acquaintance** (Affection 1–25): small talk, learn 1 interest. Journal entry appears.
3. **Friend** (25–50): hangouts unlock, gift reactions, a personal mini-quest.
4. **Close** (50–75): their main storyline beat; a choice that moves Trust a lot.
5. **Romance/Partner OR Best friend** (75–100): confession/commitment scene, gated by stat (e.g., CHR/APP) + Trust.
6. **Endings:** partner · best friend · falling out · neutral drift.

Each interest a character likes → +bonus when talked about or gifted; disliked topics → penalty. (Drives the "get to know them" loop.)
